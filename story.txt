After years of working to perfect our menu for 
customers like you, we are happy to share with you
a menu filled with flavor that is also healthy and 
100% gluten free. The owner of Milestone Cafe has 
always struggled being able to enjoy meals out with 
friends and family without having to worry about the 
food being gluten free. Because of this they decided to 
create an atmosphere with tasty food that everyone can 
enjoy without worrying what is in the food.
